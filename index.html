Get Started

A Minecraft Imperial replica

Our amazing frontend team built in just 15 days a replica of campus in minecraft with exact ratios, scales, and colors. A full asset store is avaiable for contributions as well. We support real-time lecture links in the Minecraft lecture rooms as well as 3D audio on sepearate servers

Minigames and custom events

Our servers are scalable and support custom plugins from developers, custom rooms from any designers, and custom database additions for anything you would like to display in the world! From quizzes, to treasure hunts, your imagination is the limit! Get in touch with us to get more details about integrating your events

Add your personal touch

Think something is missing in your department? Have an amazing idea you would like to add? Want to design your lecture halls? That server is for you! We also support world guard, version control, and op management. Get in touch if you are a developer or simply a minecraft enthousiast who wants to contribute to the project
