FAQ

1. For third party commissioners.

Q: I am interested to use this Imperial virtual world as a platform for my own society events, what steps should I follow?

A: This virtual world is well developed and can be easily adapted for public events that conventionally happens on campus.
To access this vitual world, please have a look at Client Setup and Administration. For front end changes and developments, please check World Edit/Guard and contact an admin by imperial_virtual_world@gmail.com so we can grant you construction permission and provide related support.
If you would like to hold it on your own server, there might be some administration issues and please contact imperial_virtual_world@gmail.com for further details. Technically it is easy to set up a separate copy of this virtual world on another server.
